Summer Showreel

You could play 'name that tune' while you listen.

Snippets of many of my songs can be seen in my 'Andy B Goode Summer Showreel 2016'. This was recorded live at St Pauls Church Dosthill Summer Vintage Fete.

Teenager In Love

It's great to hear people joining in at the chorus. This was recorded at the Dosthill Summer Vintage Fayre. I also performed this song on on TCR FM at the 'Norton Juxta Twycross Summer Fete.

Johnny B. Goode

Feel free to sing along.
'Johnny B. Goode' originally by the fantastic Chuck Berry. 'Back to the Future' is one of my favourite movies, and this track really adds energy to the film.

Earth Angel

'Earth Angel' from originally by 'The Penguins'. I just keep singing songs from the 'Back to the Future' movie.

Dont Sit Under The Apple Tree 'Live'

The Jays Vintage (Vintage Vocal Harmony Tribute Group) performs the Andrews Sister's classic, "Don't Sit Under The Apple Tree". This was recorded live at cute Red Carpet Cinema at Barton Marina.

In The Mood 'Live'

The Jays Vintage (Vintage Vocal Harmony Tribute Group) performs the Andrews Sister's classic, "In The Mood" and it certainly was mood lighting that night. This was recorded live at Red Carpet Cinema at Barton Marina.

Santa Claus Is Coming To Town 'Live'

The Jays Vintage (Vintage Vocal Harmony Tribute Group) performs the Perry Como Christmas classic, "Santa Claus is Coming To Town". This was recorded live at Red Carpet Cinema at Barton Marina.
