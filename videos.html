1950s/60s Rock n Roll Jukebox

Here's some of my Rock and Roll hits, including: Hound Dog, Johnny B. Goode, Teenager in Love, This Ole House, Great Balls of Fire and more. Feel free to sing along.
'Johnny B. Goode' originally by the fantastic Chuck Berry. 'Back to the Future' is one of my favourite movies, and that track really adds energy to the film.

Summer Showreel

You could play 'Name that Tune' while you listen.

Snippets of many of my songs can be found in the 'Andy B Goode Summer Showreel 2016'. This was recorded live at St Pauls Church Dosthill Summer Vintage Fete. Enjoy!

Christmas Playlist

Christmas is a special time for music. Here's a few classics including hits by Chuck Berry, Dean Martin and Michael Buble: Run Rudolph Run, Frosty the Snowman, Winter Wonderland, Jingle Bells, Holly Jolly Christmas, Rockin Robin, Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer, White Christmas, Silent Night and The Christmas Song.

Fly Me To The Moon

The definitive Frank Sinatra song from the time when holidays on jets liners were new. This was recorded live at norton Juxta Twycross Fete.

You Make Me Feel So Young

Another Frank Sinatra tune about his love for his favourite girl. All those times that make life worth living.

How Do You Like Your Eggs in the Morning

A Dean Martin song performed by Andy B Goode and Sophie Dangerfield. What is a morning on BBC 2 without it.

Beer Barrel Polka

The Jays Vintage (Vintage Vocal Harmony Tribute Group) performs the classic, "Beer Barrel Polka".

Magic Moments

Dean Martins song remembering those little events that make life special.
